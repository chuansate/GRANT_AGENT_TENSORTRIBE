Program Geran Pemadanan Change Upgrade Product (CUP) is considered for each entrepreneur based on the following criteria:
a. Incorporated under the Companies Act 2016 [Act 777] or registered under the Registration of Businesses Act 1956 [Act 197]
b. Fishermen associations registered under the Fishermen Associations Act 1971
c. Farmers organizations registered under the Constitution of the Farmers' Organization
d. Cooperatives registered under the Cooperative Societies Act 1993 [Act 502]
e. Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) registered under the Societies Act 1966
f. Possesses a valid business license
g. Operates from a business premise with a valid premises license
h. Operates from a business premise or designated business space with a valid premises license
i. Participants who have successfully completed and been monitored for at least one year under the Azam Tani Project, Rezeki Tani Program, and Young Agropreneur Program
