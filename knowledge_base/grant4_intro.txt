Program Geran Pemadanan Change Upgrade Product (CUP) is considered for food and agro-based industry entrepreneurs in the small category with an annual gross sales value of less than RM300,000.
This grant offers assistance under the following scopes:
- Product quality development studies
- Packaging and labeling design
- Production capacity enhancement
- Premises improvement
- Procurement of business signage

The government will fund the total project cost (including Sales and Service Tax) for each application at the following rates:
- 100% up to a maximum of RM1,000 under the Product Quality Development Study scope
- 70% up to a maximum of RM60,000 under the other scopes
